Photography In Nature

I feel most at peace with myself and with life itself when I'm standing in front of landscapes with rocks, trees, reflecting water, and parting clouds. I feel compelled to make an effort to convey the sensation of amazement I experience visually; I have a feeling that you can relate to this.
